## Purpose

This file defines the HAPS Solar Electric Development (HAPS-SE-DEV) programme governed under AEROSPACEMODEL.

## Programme Description

The HAPS-SE-DEV programme governs the development, certification, and entry-into-service of the HAPS Solar Electric platform variant from concept through to operational deployment.

## Programme Scope

- Development of the HAPS-SE solar-electric stratospheric platform
- System requirements definition and architecture design
- Structural and aerodynamic analysis of ultra-high AR wing and twin-boom configuration
- Solar energy system sizing and energy balance validation
- Distributed electric propulsion system development
- Payload integration for Comms, EO, and ISR missions
- Regulatory engagement for ICAO gap compliance and ITU spectrum coordination
- Entry into service and initial operational deployment
