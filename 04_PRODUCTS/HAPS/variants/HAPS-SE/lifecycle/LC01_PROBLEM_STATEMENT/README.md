## Phase Purpose

Define the operational need, mission context, and problem space for the HAPS Solar Electric platform.

The HAPS-SE addresses the need for persistent, low-cost stratospheric connectivity and sensing capability over wide areas without the recurring cost and orbital complexity of satellite systems.

## Problem Statement Summary

The operational gap addressed by HAPS-SE is the lack of persistent, wide-area, affordable communications and sensing coverage at stratospheric altitude where:

- Satellite systems provide high latency and limited spot coverage
- Conventional aircraft have insufficient endurance (hours vs. weeks/months required)
- Ground infrastructure is absent in maritime, polar, and developing-region areas

HAPS-SE fills this gap through solar-powered, station-keeping stratospheric flight.
