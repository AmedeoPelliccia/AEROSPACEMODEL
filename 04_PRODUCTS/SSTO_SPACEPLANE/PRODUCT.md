## Purpose

Defines the SSTO_SPACEPLANE product family governed under AEROSPACEMODEL.

## Product Family Description

Single-Stage-To-Orbit (SSTO) reusable spaceplane for orbital access.
Integrated airframe-propulsion vehicle with metallic/CMC Thermal Protection
System (TPS), operating from Mach 0 through orbital insertion. Cryogenic
LH₂/LOX propellant architecture. Combined-cycle or rocket propulsion.
Designed for horizontal runway take-off and landing with full reusability.

This assessment addresses the **orbital SSTO mission only**. It does not
treat hypersonic point-to-point transport as an equivalent mission class.
