## Purpose

Defines the SSTO_SPACEPLANE product family governed under AEROSPACEMODEL.

## Product Family Description

Single-Stage-To-Orbit (SSTO) spaceplane configured for hypersonic transport.
Waverider-derived airframe with metallic Thermal Protection System (TPS),
operating across Mach 0 to 25+. Shock system feeds the inlet. Combined-cycle
or rocket propulsion using LH₂/LOX propellants. Designed for runway take-off
and runway landing with full reusability.
