## Purpose

This file defines the HAPS_SE (High Altitude Pseudo-Satellite — Solar Electric)
product family governed under AEROSPACEMODEL.

## Product Family Description

HAPS-SE addresses the persistent coverage gap between conventional aircraft
ceiling (~14 km / FL450) and low-Earth-orbit satellite altitude (~200+ km).
The platform operates autonomously in the lower stratosphere (18–22 km) using
solar-electric propulsion with rechargeable energy storage, enabling multi-month
station-keeping without fuel resupply.

Target mission profiles include:

- **Earth observation** — persistent wide-area surveillance and mapping
- **Telecommunications relay** — 4G/5G backhaul, IoT connectivity, emergency comms
- **Atmospheric science** — in-situ stratospheric measurement campaigns
- **Surveillance & reconnaissance** — defence and border security applications
