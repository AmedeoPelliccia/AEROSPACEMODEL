## Retention Periods

| Evidence Type | Retention Period |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Certification evidence | Life of product + 10 years |
| Safety assessment records | Life of product + 10 years |
| Test reports | 10 years from creation |
| Audit records | 10 years from closure |
| Training records | Employment duration + 5 years |
| Other evidence | 7 years minimum |

## Archive Requirements

Evidence must be archived in a format accessible for the full retention period.
