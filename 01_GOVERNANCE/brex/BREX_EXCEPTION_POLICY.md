## Purpose

Defines the process for granting exceptions to BREX rules.

## Rules

1. BREX ERROR rule exceptions require ASIT approval.
2. Exceptions must be documented with justification and expiry date.
3. Exceptions are recorded in a controlled exception register.
4. BREX WARNING exceptions can be approved by QA Authority.
5. No permanent exceptions to safety-critical BREX rules.
