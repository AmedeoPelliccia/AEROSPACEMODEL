**Authority:** ASIT  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## Steps

1. Author identifies required deviation or waiver
2. Formal request submitted with justification and risk assessment
3. QA Authority reviews request
4. ASIT approval required for deviations from controlled objects
5. Certification Authority approval for certification-impacting deviations
6. Approved deviation/waiver recorded with expiry date
7. Monitoring of deviation until resolution or formal waiver grant
