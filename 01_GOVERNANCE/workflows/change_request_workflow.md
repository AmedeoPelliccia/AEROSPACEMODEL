**Path:** `01_GOVERNANCE/workflows/change_request_workflow.md`  
**Authority:** ASIT  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## Steps

1. Author creates CR with title, description, affected objects, and impact assessment
2. CR submitted to ASIT for triage
3. Impact assessment reviewed
4. CR approved or rejected by appropriate authority
5. If approved: changes implemented on feature branch
6. CI validation executed
7. Peer review completed
8. ASIT sign-off
9. Merge to governed branch
10. `CHANGELOG.md` updated
11. CR closed
