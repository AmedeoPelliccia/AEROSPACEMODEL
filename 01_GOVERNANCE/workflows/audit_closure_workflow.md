## Steps

1. Audit conducted and findings recorded
2. NCRs raised and assigned owners
3. Corrective actions defined
4. Corrective actions implemented and verified
5. Audit lead confirms closure of all NCRs
6. Audit Authority signs off closure
7. Audit record updated to CLOSED status
8. Lessons learned recorded
