## Purpose

Defines the rules for maintaining repository structural and content integrity.

## Rules

1. The canonical folder structure defined in `AEROSPACEMODEL.md` must be maintained.
2. All mandatory files must be present and valid.
3. CI structural validation must pass before merging.
4. Orphan KNUs must be resolved or explicitly exempted.
5. No controlled object may be deleted without a formal CR and ASIT approval.
