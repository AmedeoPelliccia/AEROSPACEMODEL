**Authority:** ASIT  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## Purpose

Defines the rules for managing objective evidence within AEROSPACEMODEL.

## Rules

1. All evidence must be assigned an OID and registered in `03_SHARED_SERVICES/evidence/evidence_register.csv`.
2. Evidence must be addressable via a controlled path or external reference.
3. Evidence must be linked to at least one KNU.
4. Evidence retention requirements are defined in `03_SHARED_SERVICES/evidence/evidence_retention_policy.md`.
5. Acceptance criteria for evidence types are defined in `03_SHARED_SERVICES/evidence/evidence_acceptance_criteria.yaml`.
