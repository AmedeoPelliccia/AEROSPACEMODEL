## Rules

1. Digital signatures are accepted in lieu of wet signatures for repository governed objects.
2. Signatures must be traceable to a named individual and role.
3. Signoff records must include: name, role, date, and object reference.
4. Signoff records are immutable once recorded.
5. Corrections require a new signoff record with supersession reference.
