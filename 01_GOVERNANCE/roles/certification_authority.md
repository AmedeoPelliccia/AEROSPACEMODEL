## Purpose

The Certification Authority role is responsible for compliance and certification sign-off.

## Responsibilities

- Review and approve certification evidence packages
- Sign off compliance status for regulatory obligations
- Maintain certification baseline records
- Liaise with regulatory authorities (EASA, FAA, etc.)
