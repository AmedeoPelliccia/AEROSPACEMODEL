## Purpose

The QA Authority role is responsible for quality assurance sign-off across the lifecycle.

## Responsibilities

- Review and approve quality-related KNUs and evidence
- Sign off quality audits and NCRs
- Approve CAPA records
- Maintain QMS compliance mapping
