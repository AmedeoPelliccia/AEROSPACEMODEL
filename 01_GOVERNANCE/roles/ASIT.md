## Purpose

The Authoritative System Integration and Traceability (ASIT) role is the primary governing authority for repository integrity.

## Responsibilities

- Define and maintain repository structure, naming conventions, and controlled vocabulary
- Approve changes to governance documents, schemas, and controlled registries
- Ensure lifecycle traceability chain integrity
- Resolve orphan KNU classifications
- Sign off baselines and major releases
- Authorize exemptions from mapping requirements
