# M2-MAINTENANCE_ENVIRONMENTS Index

## Subdomain Overview

**M2 — Maintenance Environments** covers all ground-based infrastructure required to maintain and service aircraft, including leveling, weighing, parking, storage, and full-spectrum servicing.
