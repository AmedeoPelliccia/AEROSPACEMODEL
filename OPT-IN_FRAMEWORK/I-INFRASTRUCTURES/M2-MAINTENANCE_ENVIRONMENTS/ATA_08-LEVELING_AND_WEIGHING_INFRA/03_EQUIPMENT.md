# ATA 08I — Leveling and Weighing Infrastructure: Equipment Catalog

## 1. Purpose

This document catalogs all equipment required to perform leveling and weighing operations for the AMPEL360 Q100 aircraft at maintenance facilities.

---

## 6. ⭐ Special Condition: H₂ Aircraft Equipment

For AMPEL360 Q100 with LH₂ fuel systems:

| Item | Description | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Cryogenic temperature reader | For LH₂ tank temperature display during weighing | ⭐ SC-28-H2-001 |
| LH₂ tank pressure gauge | For fuel density determination | ⭐ SC-28-H2-001 |
| H₂ personal gas detector | Worn by each operator during H₂ aircraft weighing | ⭐ Mandatory |
| PPE kit (H₂ adjacent) | Face shield, cryogenic gloves (if H₂ lines exposed) | ⭐ Per ATA 28 |

> ⭐ **Note:** H₂ aircraft weighing shall only be performed with aircraft fuel system isolated and pressurized to ≤ storage pressure. Active LH₂ transfer is prohibited during weighing operations.

## 8. Equipment Approval Status

All weighing equipment used for official W&B records shall have:

- [ ] Valid calibration certificate (within calibration interval)
- [ ] EASA Part-145 shop equipment approval (if at approved MRO)
- [ ] Entry in facility equipment register
- [ ] Last calibration date tag attached to equipment
