| Service | ATA Sub-Section | Equipment | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| LH₂ refuelling (standard) | 12-50 | LH₂ servicing cart (≥ 500 L Dewar) | Per `04_PROCEDURES.md` 12-PRO-002 |
