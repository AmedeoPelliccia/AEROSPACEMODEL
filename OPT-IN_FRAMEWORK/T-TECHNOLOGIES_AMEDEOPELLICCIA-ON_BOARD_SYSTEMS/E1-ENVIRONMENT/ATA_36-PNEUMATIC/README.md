**Domain:** T-TECHNOLOGIES / E1-ENVIRONMENT
**Boundary:** SHARED
**Doc ID:** T-E1-ATA36-001
**Lifecycle:** Standard (LC01–LC13)

## Scope

Aircraft pneumatic power distribution for the AMPEL360 Q100. Routes bleed air or electrically-generated pneumatic supply to ATA 21, ATA 30, and other consumers. Manages pressure regulation and duct leak protection.
